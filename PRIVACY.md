# Privacy Policy for G-Suite Dark Mode

This extension is built with a **Privacy-First** philosophy. Since this tool is designed to enhance your productivity, we believe your data should remain entirely your own.

## 1. Data Collection
- **No Personal Data:** This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, email addresses, or Google account data.
- **No Browsing History:** We do not track your browsing habits or history. The extension only activates its CSS filters when you explicitly interact with it on authorized Google domains.

## 2. Data Usage
- **Local Processing:** All "Dark Mode" processing happens locally on your machine. The extension injects a CSS stylesheet into your current tab to change the visual appearance, and this data never leaves your browser.
- **No Third-Parties:** We do not share, sell, or trade any information with third-party companies or advertisers.

## 3. Permissions Explained
To function correctly, the extension requests the following permissions:
- `scripting`: Required to inject the dark mode CSS into the page.
- `activeTab`: Required to apply the theme only when you click the extension icon.
- `host_permissions`: Limited to Google domains (e.g., `docs.google.com`) to ensure the extension only runs where it is needed.

## 4. Updates
This policy may be updated to reflect changes in Chrome Extension requirements (Manifest V3), but the core principle—**zero data collection**—will never change.
